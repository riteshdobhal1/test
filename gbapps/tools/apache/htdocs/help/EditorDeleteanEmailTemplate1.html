Edit or Delete an Email Template

  1. Click the Manage Templates link. Scroll through the templates list.
  2. Select the edit icon. Make appropriate changes as required and click Submit. 
  3. Select the delete icon to remove the template from the repository. 

Standard Subject and attributes

This section lists attributes of a log that you can use in the subject and body of email notifications. These attributes will be replaced by its actual value corresponding to the log file for which the rule is triggered. For example, {cust_name} generates the end customer name specific to a log bundle.

Setting up Email Alert Notifications

An email will be sent to any users that are configured to receive a notification on a triggered event. 

Note: The E-mail recipient has to be in the same domain as your company’s domain. That is, if your company’s domain is dyk.com and that is how you are registered into Glassbeam, then you can only add email addresses from dyk.com domain. You cannot add email ids of gmail.com, yahoo.com etc. as E-mail recipients for alerts.
