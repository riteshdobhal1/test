About Glassbeam


Glassbeam is a log data analytics company that provides cloud based solutions for analyzing any log from any device.  

Machine logs contain critical, high value business data.  Glassbeam was founded in order to leverage the need to mine this data and thereby provide a competitive edge to the product owners and IT users alike.

Glassbeam has created an evolutionary new platform to collect, refine, analyze, and present product operational data in compelling and visually appealing dashboards.  Glassbeam helps user's support teams to troubleshoot faster, engineering teams to build better products, and sales teams to increase revenues.
