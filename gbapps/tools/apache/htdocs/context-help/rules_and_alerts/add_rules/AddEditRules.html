    Attribute_List

    • Search specific attributes from a long attribute list.
    • Reduce the time to scan through the attributes in a long list.  

    Steps to follow for searching attributes:

    1. Enter attribute name as search criteria.  A minimum of 2 characters of the attribute name must be entered.
    2. Press Enter or click on the search button to display the list of attributes according to the search criteria.

    Example:

    • Enter abc as the attribute name to be searched.
    • All sections with attributes that have abc as part of the attribute name are listed.  
    • Click on  to expand the section to list all its attributes.
